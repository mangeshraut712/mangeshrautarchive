About Mangesh Raut

Software engineer portfolio of an AI builder and product thinker — from Drexel University in Philadelphia to production systems.

MS in Computer Science

Hi, I'm Mangesh Raut — software engineer.

I’m a software engineer, AI builder, and lifelong learner who enjoys turning ambitious ideas into real products. This site is my personal software engineer portfolio and resume home.

Originally from India and educated in the United States, I earned a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Drexel University and have spent years building software across web applications, artificial intelligence, cloud platforms, automation systems, and developer tools — including Java, Python, AWS, and full stack delivery.

What excites me most isn’t technology itself—it’s what technology enables.

Whether it’s an AI system that makes knowledge more accessible, a platform that helps people make better decisions, or a product that saves someone hours of work, I enjoy building things that solve real problems and create measurable impact.

Today, much of my work focuses on artificial intelligence, large language models, agentic systems, developer productivity tools, and full-stack software engineering. Recent projects include HindAI, an AI-powered platform built on Google’s Gemma models to explore Indian knowledge systems and philosophy, AI-enhanced web applications, intelligent assistants, and modern digital experiences inspired by Apple’s design philosophy.

I believe the future belongs to builders who can combine engineering, creativity, and product thinking. The best software isn’t just technically impressive—it feels intuitive, useful, and human.

Outside of software, interests include cricket, fitness, health optimization, emerging technologies, entrepreneurship, and understanding how AI can be used responsibly to improve people’s lives.

Every project is an opportunity to learn something new. Every challenge is a chance to build something better.
